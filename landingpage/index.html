Tour Itinerary
Day 01: Dehradun / Haridwar – Sersi / Guptkashi (210 kms / 07 hrs drive)

Pick up from Dehradun / Haridwar and proceed to Guptkashi (It’s situated at a distance of 47 km from the holy shrine, Kedarnath) The town holds immense religious importance as it houses famous ancient temples like Vishwanath Temple and Ardhnareshwar Temple. Have your dinner and stay overnight in the hotel/camp.

Day 02: Guptkashi / Sersi – Kedarnath – Badrinath - Guptkashi / Sersi Early morning drive to helipad for Kedarnath - Badrinath Yatra by Helicopter.

Day 03: Guptkashi / Sersi – Rishikesh – Dehradun / Haridwar (210 kms / 07 hrs drive)

Early morning, after breakfast, you drive downhill to Dehradun / Haridwar via Rishikesh a spiritual city and the Yoga capital of the world. Enroute visit Ram Jhula and Laxman Jhula. Later drive to Dehradun / Haridwar.
